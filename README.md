## Company Background (Wikipedia snapshot)
NVIDIA Corporation is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. Founded on April 5 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky and Curtis Priem, NVIDIA pioneered the graphics processing unit (GPU) and later expanded into data‑center AI accelerators, autonomous‑machine platforms, and professional visualization. Key milestones include the GeForce series, CUDA (2006), Mellanox acquisition (2020), and the Hopper/Blackwell AI architectures (2023‑2024). As of 2025, ~87 % of revenue comes from Compute & Networking (AI/data‑center).
